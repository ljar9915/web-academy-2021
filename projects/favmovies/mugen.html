movie summary

demon slayer the movie: mugen train is a continuation of the first season of "demon slayer: kimetsu no yaiba". the movie is about 4 demon slayers who board the mugen train on a hunt to kill enmu, a demon that has been killing many of their kind. tanjiro, his sister nezuko (who is a passive demon), zenitsu, & inosuke join the flame hashira Rengoku on the hunt. i won't spoil what happens, but just know around 80% of the movie consists of epic action/battle scenes.

demon slayer: mugen train is one of my favorite movies because first of all, i love the anime (demon slayer: kimetsu no yaiba) that the movie is based off. i remember binge-watching the entire 23-episode season of demon slayer in 2-3 days. the plot is amazingly intricate & the characters will have you hooked in no time. the animation of the battle scenes especially in this movie is absolutely nothing short of insane. i was left mesmerized on the edge of my theater seat the entire time.

i chose green, black, & red for the color scheme of this webpage to match tanjiro, the protagonist of demon slayer's color palette. tanjiro has red hair & a green & black checkered jacket.
