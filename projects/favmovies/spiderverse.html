movie #1

spiderman: into the spider-verse

movie summary

spiderman: into the spiderverse is a movie about miles morales, a regular teenage student who one day gets bitten by a radioactive spider & starts gaining superpowers. soon, spider-people from other universes begin to be pulled into miles' universe by the evil Kingpin. with the help of peter b. parker, gwen stacy, & other spider-people, miles has to take down kingpin in order to stop the multiverse from colliding & get his friends back home.

into the spiderverse is my favorite movie of all time for a lot of reasons. the art style, from the character design to the animation to the landscaping, is so unique & inspires my own art style a lot even years after the movie came out. i also adore all of the characters' different personalities, especially miles & gwen. the plot & storyline is something that i never get tired of- the scene where miles finally learns to control his powers is so powerful to me. on top of all that, one of the most perfect movie soundtracks i've ever been blessed to listen to. i love this movie so much.

i chose red, black & white for the color scheme of this webpage because miles' spider suit is black with red spray paint designs & white eyes.
